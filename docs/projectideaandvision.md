Suppose you become a freelancer.

You have:

XYZ asks you to build a mobile app.

Now your life becomes messy.

* Which client paid me?
* Which project is due tomorrow?
* Which tasks are pending?
* Which invoice is unpaid?
* Where is the contract PDF?
* How many hours did I work?

Many freelancers end up using:

* Notes app
* Excel
* WhatsApp
* Google Drive
* Calendar

Everything is scattered.
